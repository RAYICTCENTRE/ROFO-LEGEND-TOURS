Bird Watching in Uganda

Spot rare and exotic birds across Uganda’s forests, wetlands, and savannahs. Ideal for bird lovers and wildlife photographers.

Best Locations

Kibale Forest, Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Queen Elizabeth National Park, and Mabamba Swamp offer exceptional birding opportunities.

Species to Spot

Look for Shoebill storks, African Grey Parrots, Turacos, and over 1,000 recorded species across Uganda.

Tips

Bring binoculars, a field guide, and comfortable walking shoes. Early mornings are best for active sightings.
