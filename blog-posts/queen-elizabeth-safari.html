Queen Elizabeth National Park Safari

Discover the wildlife and beauty of Queen Elizabeth National Park, home to tree-climbing lions, elephants, and abundant wildlife.

Wildlife Highlights

Game drives offer sightings of lions, leopards, elephants, hippos, buffaloes, and over 600 bird species.

Activities

Boat cruises on the Kazinga Channel, guided safaris, bird watching, and cultural encounters with local communities.

Best Time to Visit

June–September provides dry conditions, ideal for game viewing and photography.
