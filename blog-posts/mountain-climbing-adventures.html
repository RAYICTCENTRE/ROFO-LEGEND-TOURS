Mountain Climbing & Hiking Adventures

Challenge yourself on Uganda’s mountains, including the Rwenzori range and scenic peaks, for unforgettable panoramic views.

Popular Mountains

Rwenzori Mountains, Mount Elgon, and Sipi Falls areas offer trails for beginners and experienced climbers alike.

Preparation

Bring hiking boots, water, warm clothing, and follow guides’ instructions for safe climbing experiences.

Adventure Tips

Early morning hikes give better weather and lighting for photography. Always respect nature and local communities.
