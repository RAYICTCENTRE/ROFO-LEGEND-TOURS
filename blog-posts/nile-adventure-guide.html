Adventure on the Nile River

Feel the thrill of rafting, kayaking, and other adventure activities on the mighty Nile River in Jinja, Uganda.

Rafting & Kayaking

Experience class III–V rapids with expert guides. Suitable for both beginners and experienced adventurers.

Safety Tips

Always wear life jackets and follow guides’ instructions. Morning trips offer calmer waters and better visibility.

Other Activities

Enjoy bungee jumping, zip-lining, and scenic boat rides along the river for a full adventure experience.
