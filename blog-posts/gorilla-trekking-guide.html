Gorilla Trekking in Uganda

Step into the misty forests of Bwindi and Mgahinga to meet majestic mountain gorillas. Discover tips, what to bring, and how to make your safari unforgettable.

Planning Your Trek

Gorilla trekking permits must be booked in advance. Wear comfortable hiking shoes, bring water, snacks, and a camera. Follow guides and respect the animals’ space.

Best Seasons

June–September and December–February offer dry trails and easier tracking. Early morning treks provide the best encounters.

What to Expect

Guided treks through lush forests, spotting birds and other wildlife. Witness gorillas up close in a natural habitat, creating unforgettable memories.
