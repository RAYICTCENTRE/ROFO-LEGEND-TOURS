Murchison Falls Safari

Discover the roaring Murchison Falls, breathtaking landscapes, and abundant wildlife with our guided safari tours.

Wildlife Highlights

Lions, elephants, hippos, giraffes, and countless bird species inhabit this national park. Game drives and boat safaris provide excellent viewing opportunities.

Boat Cruises

Enjoy serene Nile river cruises at the base of the falls, spotting hippos and crocodiles while taking in spectacular scenery.

Photography Tips

Bring a zoom lens for wildlife, and shoot during golden hours (sunrise/sunset) for dramatic landscapes.
